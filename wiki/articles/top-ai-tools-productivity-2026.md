## Recommended Stack (Most Productive Users)

1. Claude or ChatGPT — heavy thinking + writing
2. Notion AI — documentation
3. Perplexity — quick cited research
4. Otter.ai — meeting intelligence
5. Zapier AI — connecting everything

---

## Key Concepts

- **Claude's 200K context window** is the decisive differentiator for document-heavy work
- **ChatGPT's plugin ecosystem** is unmatched — hundreds of connectors
- **Notion AI integration advantage:** No copy-paste context switching
- **Otter.ai ROI:** 3 hours of meetings/day → eliminates note-taking entirely
- **Recommended onboarding:** Pick one tool, use it for 30 days, then layer in the next based on biggest remaining bottleneck

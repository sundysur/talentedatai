## Summary

Maps the ideal AI tool to each of six stages of the blogging pipeline: Ideation → Research → Outline → Draft → SEO Optimization → Final Edit. Provides a full cost breakdown (full stack ~$164/month; budget version ~$40/month). Emphasises that the final edit by a human is non-negotiable.

---

## Six-Stage Pipeline

| Stage | Best Tool | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Ideation | ChatGPT + AnswerThePublic/Semrush | $20/month |
| Research | Perplexity AI | $20/month |
| Outline | Claude | $20/month |
| First Draft | Claude (solo) / Jasper (teams) | $20–$49+/month |
| SEO Optimization | Surfer SEO / Frase.io (budget) | $89/$15/month |
| Final Edit | Human (Hemingway + Grammarly) | Free/$15/month |

---

## Key Concepts

- **Stages, not tools:** AI should be applied per stage, not generically.
- **Claude for outlining:** More thorough and better structured for long-form than ChatGPT.
- **Surfer SEO:** Matches keyword frequency and structure to top-ranking pages; Frase.io is the budget alternative.
- **Final human edit is essential:** Voice adjustment, fact-checking, personal examples, and pruning verbosity.
- **The differentiator:** Top AI bloggers scale their thinking, not replace it.

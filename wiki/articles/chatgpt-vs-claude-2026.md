## Decision Guide

- **Choose Claude:** Long-form writing, document analysis, nuanced reasoning, less hallucination
- **Choose ChatGPT:** Image generation (DALL-E 3), plugins, coding, voice mode, video analysis
- **Use both:** Agencies, diverse AI needs; ~$40/month total is worth it for most professionals

---

## Key Concepts

- **200K token context (Claude):** Decisive advantage for 50+ page document analysis
- **Plugin ecosystem (ChatGPT):** Hundreds of plugins; Claude has none as of test date
- **Hallucination:** Claude was more willing to admit uncertainty; ChatGPT occasionally produced confident wrong answers
- **Recommendation:** Start with Claude for most knowledge workers; add ChatGPT when needed

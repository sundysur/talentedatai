## Summary

Comprehensive review of 10 AI video generators tested with consistent prompts across three categories: text-to-video, avatar/presenter, and hybrid editing platforms. Major finding: the categories are converging — choose by use case, not tool type.

## Key Concepts

- **Quality tiers now matter more than categories:** The divide is control vs. no control, not text-to-video vs. avatar.
- **Temporal consistency:** Kling 3.0's key differentiator — subjects don't morph between frames.
- **Convergence trend:** Editing platforms (CapCut) now embed generation models; generator tools add editing features.
- **Open-source option (Wan 2.6):** Requires 16GB VRAM; zero marginal cost per generation.

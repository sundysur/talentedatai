## Key Concepts

- **Lowest risk start:** Method 1 (existing skill) or Method 2 (long-term asset building)
- **Specificity sells:** "50 ChatGPT prompts for real estate agents" >> "100 AI prompts"
- **Generic AI output is worthless in 2026:** Competition is high; quality + judgment are the differentiators
- **YouTube automation stack:** Claude (script) + ElevenLabs (voice) + Descript/InVideo (edit) + Midjourney (thumbnails)

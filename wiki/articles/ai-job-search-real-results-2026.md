## Summary

A practical, honest guide to using AI in every phase of the job search. Covers cover letter drafting (AI removes blank page; human adds voice), resume tailoring (reframing real experience with job-matched language), mock interview practice (Claude gives layered, specific feedback), and company research (Perplexity for current, cited competitive intel). Recommends a simple two-tool stack: Claude/ChatGPT + Perplexity.

---

## Key Concepts

- **AI as first-draft tool, not ghostwriter:** The goal is the clearest, most prepared version of yourself.
- **Cover letter workflow:** Paste JD + experience summary → AI draft → humanise, add voice, cut filler.
- **Resume tailoring:** AI identifies top 10 priorities from JD, then re-frames existing experience to match.
- **Mock interview via Claude:** Ask for the hardest version of behavioural questions; Claude gives specific (not generic) feedback.
- **Company research via Perplexity:** Real-time, cited intelligence on competitive landscape, culture, recent news.
- **What AI can't do:** Make you a stronger candidate than you are, or do the emotional labour of job searching.

## Summary

Honest review of ElevenLabs, the category leader in AI voice generation. Best for podcasters, bloggers adding audio, and small businesses replacing studio voiceover. Voice cloning with 3-minute sample passes informal listening tests. Dubbing works for simple content. Not yet a replacement where emotional authenticity is essential.

---

## Features Tested

- **Pre-built voices:** Hundreds of options; quality varies widely; best ones are remarkably natural; worst have a "smoothed" artificiality. Emotional nuance is the hardest gap.
- **Voice cloning:** Most impressive feature. 3-minute clean sample produces a convincing clone. Requires ~1 minute minimum; longer = better.
- **Dubbing:** Translates video preserving original voice characteristics. Works well for simple content; not ready for complex audio/fast-paced content.

## Best Use Cases

- **Podcasters:** Consistent voice for intros, ads, templated segments
- **Bloggers/Newsletter writers:** Audio versions of articles in 5 minutes
- **Small businesses:** Replace studio voiceover for routine content
- **Language learners:** Authentic regional accent samples for ear training
- **YouTube automation:** ElevenLabs is the voice layer in faceless channel workflow

---

## Limitations

- Pacing and emphasis still slip on complex or emotional content
- Pre-built voices need careful selection — quality varies
- Dubbing not ready for professional-grade use

## Summary

Practical task-based comparison across 6 categories. Claude wins on writing quality, reasoning, and coding. Gemini wins on web search, multimodal inputs, and Google Workspace integration. Pricing is nearly identical (~$20/month). Recommends using both for different tasks.

## Decision Guide

- **Choose Claude:** Writing is primary use case, long documents, nuanced reasoning, complex coding
- **Choose Gemini:** Google Workspace users, need real-time web search, regular image/media processing
- **Use both:** Power users; free tiers of both are generous enough to run simultaneously at no cost

---

## Key Concepts

- **Context window:** Claude's 200K token window remains a structural advantage for document work
- **Google Workspace integration:** Not subtle — Gemini is embedded directly into Gmail, Docs, Sheets
- **Perplexity for research:** Article notes Perplexity still beats both for citation-heavy research

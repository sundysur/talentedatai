## AI Text-to-Speech: ElevenLabs (2026 Leader)

**Pricing:**
- Free: 10,000 characters/month
- Starter: $5/month (30,000 chars)
- Creator: $22/month (100,000 chars)

**Key capabilities:**
- Pre-built voice library (hundreds of voices; quality varies)
- **Voice cloning:** 3-minute audio sample → convincing voice clone
- **Dubbing:** Video translation preserving original voice characteristics

**Best for:** Podcasters, bloggers (audio articles), small business voiceover, YouTube automation

**Honest limitation:** Emotional nuance still falls short. Pacing and emphasis slip on complex content. Gap to human narration is narrowing but not closed.

## AI Music Generation: Suno

**What it does:** Text prompt → complete produced song (vocals, melody, lyrics, instrumentation) in ~30 seconds.

**Output quality:** Radio-adjacent for established genres (lo-fi, country pop, ambient). Weaker on complex/emotional content. Quality is highly prompt-dependent.

**Competitor:** Udio (different aesthetic, same category)

**Use cases:**
- Educators: custom classroom songs
- Indie devs: bespoke game/video tracks
- Musicians: compositional raw material
- Casual: birthday songs, personal creations

**Hard ethical questions:**
- Trained on recorded music without artist consent or compensation
- Active legal disputes, unresolved
- Economic harm to session musicians, jingle writers, library composers
- Does not threaten live music or artist-as-identity — only functional audio

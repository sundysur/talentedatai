## The 2026 Content Landscape

- **60% of Google searches** end without a click (AI Overviews answer directly)
- **AI Overviews** appear for ~99.9% of informational queries
- Brands cited in AI Overviews see downstream **branded search increases**
- Google has **heavily penalised thin AI content** since 2024 — generic output does not rank
- The goal is to be **cited**, not just to rank

---

## Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)

Structuring content so AI models (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) cite it:
- Clear descriptive subheadings (not clever ones)
- Inline definitions of key terms
- TL;DR or summary near the top
- Direct answers to questions your subheadings imply
- Cited sources for statistics
- Shorter paragraphs

## The Six-Stage Blogging Pipeline

1. **Ideation** — ChatGPT + keyword tool (Semrush/AnswerThePublic)
2. **Research** — Perplexity AI
3. **Outline** — Claude
4. **Draft** — Claude (solo) / Jasper (teams)
5. **SEO Optimisation** — Surfer SEO / Frase.io
6. **Final Edit** — Human (non-negotiable)

## What Separates Good AI Content From Generic

- Specific examples AI couldn't generate
- Counter-intuitive observations from real analysis
- Verified statistics with clickable sources
- A distinct editorial voice
- Original insight — something genuinely new in the article

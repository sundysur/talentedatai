## The Google AI Strategy (2026)

- **Gemini (proprietary):** Best model kept behind a subscription; integrated deeply into Workspace
- **Gemma (open-source):** Apache 2.0 licence as competitive counter to Chinese open-weight models; developer ecosystem play
- **NotebookLM (free):** Niche but powerful research tool; expands Google's AI footprint to document-heavy workflows
- **Veo 3 (free tier):** High-quality AI video generation available free through Google's AI ecosystem

---

## Key Distinctions

| | Gemini | Gemma |
|---|---|---|
| Licence | Proprietary | Apache 2.0 (free commercial use) |
| Data | Goes to Google servers | Runs locally — no data sent |
| Best model | Gemini 3 Ultra | 27B (close to GPT-4 class) |
| Cost | Free tier + $19.99/month | Free (hardware costs only) |
| Use case | General AI assistant, Workspace | Privacy, cost, edge deployment |

---

## Gemini's Unique Advantages

- **Native real-time web search** on all tiers (Claude and ChatGPT require integrations)
- **Multimodal from ground up** — superior image/chart/audio/video processing
- **Google Workspace integration** — embedded in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Drive

---

## NotebookLM's Unique Feature

The **Audio Overview** — uploads become a 10–20 minute podcast-style discussion between 2 AI hosts. Source-grounded, no hallucinations from outside data. Free.

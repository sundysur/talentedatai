## The Beginner's Psychological Barrier

*From how-to-get-started:* The real barrier isn't knowing which button to press. It's the feeling that everyone else already gets it — that you'll ask something stupid or waste an hour confused.

**Key truths:**
- AI tools are designed to be forgiving
- You cannot break them
- The worst outcome is a non-helpful answer — then you ask again differently

---

## First Steps (No Technical Knowledge Required)

1. Go to chat.openai.com or claude.ai — create a free account
2. Ask something real from your own life (not a test prompt)
3. Notice: the quality of the answer depends on the specificity of your question

---

## Three Things to Try in Week One

1. **Write a difficult message** — awkward email to landlord, professional follow-up
2. **Explain something confusing** — lease clause, medical term, news story
3. **Summarise a long article** — paste text or URL; ask for key points

---

## What to Ignore at First

- How LLMs technically work
- Future-of-work discourse and job displacement fears
- Which model is "the best" — pick one for 30 days
- Jargon: tokens, temperature, RAG, embeddings

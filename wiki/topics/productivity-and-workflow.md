## The "Stack" Approach

*From top-ai-tools-productivity-2026:* "No single tool wins every category. Start with Claude or ChatGPT — pick one and go deep for 30 days. Then layer in the next tool based on your biggest remaining bottleneck."

## Agentic Workflow Blueprints

**Lead Sniper (Marketing)**
- Zapier monitors LinkedIn keywords → scores company → drafts email → routes to CRM

**Executive Summary Dashboard (Ops)**
- Agent queries GA4 + Gmail + Asana → posts daily 5-bullet Slack summary at 5pm

**Local Compliance Agent**
- Ollama on Mac Mini reads contract PDF → compares to labour regulations → outputs flagged clauses

**Content Marketing Pipeline**
- Perplexity (research) → Claude (brief + draft) → Surfer SEO (optimise) → human edit → repurpose with Claude/ChatGPT → Buffer (schedule)

---

## Content Repurposing (1 Article → 6 Assets)

1. LinkedIn post (250 words, counter-intuitive hook, link in first comment)
2. X thread (10 tweets: hook → ideas → follow CTA)
3. Email newsletter (200-word summary for non-clickers)
4. Short video script (60 seconds: InVideo or Pictory)
5. FAQ page entry (for AI Overview citation potential)
6. Next article brief (from comments and engagement gaps)

---

## Job Search Workflow

1. Claude/ChatGPT → cover letter first draft (paste JD + experience summary)
2. AI → identify top 10 JD priorities → re-frame resume bullet points
3. Claude → mock interview with layered, specific feedback
4. Perplexity → company research with cited real-time sources

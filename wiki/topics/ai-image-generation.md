## The 2026 Field at a Glance

| Tool | Best For | Free? | Commercial Safe? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Midjourney v6** | Artistic quality, visual projects | No ($10/month min) | On paid plans |
| **Adobe Firefly 3** | Commercial work, Adobe ecosystem | Limited free | **Yes** — trained on licensed content only |
| **DALL-E 3** (ChatGPT) | Prompt accuracy, new users | Limited (via ChatGPT) | On paid plans |
| **Flux.1** | Open-source, local, volume | Free (local GPU) | Yes (open weights) |
| **Ideogram 2** | Text-in-images (posters, signage) | Generous free | On paid plans |

---

## Key Differentiators

- **Commercial IP safety:** Adobe Firefly is the only tool trained exclusively on licensed content. All others carry some risk for commercial use.
- **Prompt adherence:** DALL-E 3 reads the full prompt most faithfully. Others approximate or require prompt engineering.
- **Open-source:** Flux.1 can run locally with no subscription ceiling — best for volume/privacy.
- **Text in images:** Ideogram 2 solves a unique problem. Other tools produce decorative gibberish when asked to render readable text.

---

## Income Use Cases (from articles)

- **Stock photography:** Adobe Stock, Shutterstock now accept AI-generated images with disclosure
- **Print-on-demand:** Redbubble, Printful — upload designs, earn per item sold
- **Niche + specificity sells:** "Particular industry × demographic × aesthetic" outperforms generic imagery

---

## Decision Guide

- **Client/commercial work:** Adobe Firefly (IP safety) or Midjourney (quality)
- **Quick prompt with no learning curve:** DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT
- **Volume/local/developer:** Flux.1
- **Text must be readable in the image:** Ideogram 2 (no alternative)

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TalentedAtAI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit talentedatai.com. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

What Data We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to read articles on TalentedAtAI. However, certain data is collected automatically when you visit:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring websites and search terms used to find us
  • Browser type, operating system, and screen size (aggregated)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level, not precise)
  • Clicks on links and outbound referrals

This data is collected in aggregated, anonymised form through Google Analytics and is used solely to understand how our content is performing and to improve the site.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with TalentedAtAI. Google Analytics places cookies on your browser to collect usage data. This data is processed by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics does not identify you personally. We use it only to view aggregated traffic reports — which articles are popular, where traffic comes from, and how long readers stay on the site.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.

Google AdSense

TalentedAtAI may display advertising served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

We do not have access to or control over cookies used by third-party advertisers. Ad revenue helps fund the independent research and writing you find on this site.

Affiliate Links

Some links on TalentedAtAI are affiliate links. This means that if you click a link and purchase a product or service, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you.

We only link to products and services we have reviewed or believe are genuinely useful. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial opinions or scores. We always disclose when content contains affiliate links.

Cookies

TalentedAtAI uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics to track page views and sessions
  • Advertising cookies — set by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads

We do not use cookies to store personally identifiable information. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features of the site.

Third-Party Links

Our articles link to third-party websites (tool vendors, documentation, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal data.

Children's Privacy

TalentedAtAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via our contact page.
